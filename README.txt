World Resistance - an OpenXcom mod created by the Inventorum Development Team

This small mod, located on the Resistance lore created by Insomniac Games. Set on an alternate timeline, an aggressive invader known as Chimera has besiged much of the planet. The special US govt organization SRPA has been tasked to fight them and recover any tech that may help to win the war.
